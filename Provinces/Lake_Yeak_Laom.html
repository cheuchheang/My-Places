Sihanouk

Yeak Laom Lake is a crater lake and a tourist destination in Yeak Laom commune, Ban Lung District ក្រុងបានលុង, Ratanakiri Province ខេត្តរតនគីរី, north-eastern Cambodia. It is pronounced in English as "Yak Lom." It is about 5 kilometers south of Banlung provincial town, and is located in a protected area. In 2012, Yeak Laom Lake was selected in the list of the 15 most beautiful crater lakes in the world. The lake is encircled by a ring of forest, and the clear emerald water is used for swimming.

Note

  • life-jackets are provided when swimming
  • a small museum near it offer insight related to Tampuan culture
  • By the hill, ethnic costumes can be rent for photo shoot by the tribes there
  • Entrance fee for Cambodian is 0.25$, and for international visits is 2$
  • Food can be brought outside or buy from the stall
