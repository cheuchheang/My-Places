Koh Rong

Sihanoukville

Koh Rong is an island in the Sihanoukville Province of Cambodia. It’s known for its sandy coves and coral reefs, like those around Koh Rong Pier. Inland, the dense jungle terrain is dotted with coconut palms and waterfalls. In the south are Jewelorchids, a small zoo home to butterflies, snakes, and birds, and lively Police Beach, a party spot. High Point Rope Park has suspension bridges, rope walks and zip lines. Koh Rong's main beach…not too shabby! Despite a couple of downsides I still say that Koh Rong is most definitely worth visiting. Long Beach is no doubt one of the best beaches in Southeast Asia. Koh Rong might be touristy, but in no way is it overrun by tourists.
